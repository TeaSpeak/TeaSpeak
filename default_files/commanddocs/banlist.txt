Usage: banlist

Permissions:
  b_client_ban_list

Description:
  Displays a list of active bans on the selected virtual server.

Example:
  banlist
  banid=7 ip=1.2.3.4 created=1259444002242 invokername=Sven invokercldbid=56 invokeruid=oHhi9WzXLNEFQOwAu4JYKGU+C+c= reason enforcements=0
  error id=0 msg=ok
