Usage: clientdblist [start={offset}] [duration={limit}] [-count]

Permissions:
  b_virtualserver_client_dblist

Description:
  Displays a list of client identities known by the server including their
  database ID, last nickname, etc.

Example:
  clientdblist
  cldbid=7 client_unique_identifier=DZhdQU58qyooEK4Fr8Ly738hEmc= client_nickname=MuhChy client_created=1259147468 client_lastconnected=1259421233 ...
  error id=0 msg=ok
