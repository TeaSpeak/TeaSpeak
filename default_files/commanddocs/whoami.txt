Usage: whoami

Permissions:

Description:
  Displays information about your current ServerQuery connection including your
  loginname, etc.

Example:
  whoami
  virtualserver_status=online virtualserver_id=1 virtualserver_unique_identifier=zrPkjznB1tMnRwj01xx7RxXjqeY= client_channel_id=2 ...
  error id=0 msg=ok
